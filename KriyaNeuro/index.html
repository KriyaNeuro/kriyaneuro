KriyaNeuro Technologies Pvt Ltd

Dedicated to helping people lead fuller, healthier lives, Kriyaneuro Technologies is an R&D company that specialises in studying human brain activity and its application in developing devices and therapies for people with neural and motor disorders. We work to improve diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation for patients with various neuro-motor disabilities. Further, we also offer solutions to companies interested in improving their marketing strategies by understanding how their advertisements can affect the neural activity of potential customers.
